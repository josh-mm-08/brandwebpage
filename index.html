The Heritage

Cotton that grows
at 11,000 feet.

Pima cotton grows in the Piura region of northern Peru, in valleys shaped by the Andes and irrigated by ancient rivers. The altitude, soil, and equatorial sun produce a fibre three times the length of standard cotton — so fine it softens with every wash instead of pilling.

Our atelier sits in Lima, the King's City. Every piece is cut, sewn, and quality-checked by the same hands before it is folded, packed, and shipped directly to you in 5–8 days.

No middlemen. No markups. Just the fabric, the craft, and the distance from 11,000 feet to your door.
